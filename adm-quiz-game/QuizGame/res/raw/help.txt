Welcome to the Quiz Game!
You will have a pool of questions to answer: for each question you have some seconds for answer.
If you are unsure between 2 answer, you can press Menu->Help (in the game screen) and you will know what question is surely wrong.
Also, if you are good in questions about Science, Movie and Sports but do you don't know anything about History and Literature, you can set your preferences
clicking on Menu->Settings in the dashboard!
You can choose if you want to play with remote questions or in local, simply turning on/off your internet connection.
Enjoy the quiz game!!!
